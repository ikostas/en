I've migrated the blog

  • 11 November 2024
  • #blog

Hi everyone, a quick update about the blog. I've migrated to 11ty, maybe Python is not fun anymore. :) I'd like to mention a few upgrades (and one downgrade), which I did by the way:

  • Font Awesome instead of icons for social network links
  • Local search, powered by Pagefind, instead of Google search
  • Dark theme
  • PicoCSS
  • I didn't integrate Disqus this time

So the focus is on usability and speed, I hope you'll like it!

P.S. I really think I should change something from time to time, just for the sake of changing.
